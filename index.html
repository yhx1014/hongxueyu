Provide liquidity for digital assets and blockchain-based financial products. Improve digital assets ecology. Serve digital economy.

Provide protocol for lending DAPPs around the world. Promote efficient flow of digital assets. Reduce frictional costs and default risks. Make lending services with the lowest cost.

Excellent Design of Underlying Architecture

Standard decentralized lending process: Borrower initiate order, replenishing mortgage asset to smart contract; Intelligent matching; Lender transfers assets to smart contract; Borrower repays the borrowed asset to smart contract; The contract release mortgage assets.
Analyze lending requirements and design overall program of the digital asset network layer, the open source lending protocol layer and the DAPP layer. Six functional systems ensure safe, smooth and efficient.

Creative loan order design, Freely setting interest rate.

Completely free and peer-to-peer lending network; Borrowers and lenders are free to set borrowing and lending conditions and interest rates based on their own requirements; The loan interest rate is completely determined by the voting by each user in the network; The order book is ranked according to bidding rate of both parties and complete by biding.

Security and risk control system to ensure the robustness of the global lending network

  • Anti-cheating system
  • Asset price warning system
  • User behavior analysis system
  • Smart contract code security auditing
  • KYC&3rd party address evaluation
